Kin - Morning Review

Overview

Kin was a collaborative project to create a family morning overview TV application for a parent to quickly review their kids scheduled events, associated tasks, and reminders on their Apple TV

On every card that Kin displays, there's a clickable action to go to the next card, creating a manifested cognitive relationship between the click and the information on the card that makes remembering easier.

This was a group project and my main job was the design comps, prototyping, and animations.
